Femur rotation: the key to run fast and jump high

2025年10月27日 阅读时间：1 分钟
Wayne
Movement
Wayne's Blog • Movement • Femur Rotation

The muscles around the femur support our body in daily life. It’s the direct engine that propel our body forward (running) or upward (jumping).

What 链接到标题

The femur rotation refers to the motion of the femur rotating around the hip or the knee joint. In the following, femur rotation refers to the femur rotating forward around the knee joint.

How 链接到标题

If you want to accelerate, the leg on the ground should do the shin angle change and then femur rotation, which is the most efficient movement for acceleration. Try this:

  • Start with the lunge posture when you prepare to sprint.
  • Pull your front foot towards your hamstring. Hold it there.
  • Your back leg will first drop the shin, then the shin stops and the femur will rotate you forward.
  • Don’t push your foot.

When 链接到标题

In sports, if you have space and want to accelerate, use femur rotation. If no space, just shin drop.

All these things are my understanding of movement learned from Adarian Barr.
